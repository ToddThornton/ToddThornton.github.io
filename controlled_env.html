Controlled Environments

Whether your Cleanroom needs involves semiconductor, MEMS, biotech, medical device, hospital, pharmaceutical or life sciences technology, CleanBuild is your Cleanroom partner for the environmental integrity of your process.

We provide proven, customer-matched solutions for both small research labs and large production facilities. Our turnkey services include complete Cleanroom project management, design, engineering and installation for all ranges of environmental control:

  • Temperature and humidity controlled spaces
  • ISO Class 3-8 microelectronic cleanrooms
  • USP 797 compliant rooms
  • BSL(1-4) labs
  • URS through DQ IQ OQ pharmaceutical facilities
  • Aseptic hospital surgical suites

We're experts at both particulate prevention and contamination containment; a local partner with global experience. If you're expanding your current Cleanroom facility, or contemplating building a new one, CleanBuild can help.
